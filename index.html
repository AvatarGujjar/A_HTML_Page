About

Every corner is designed to meet your needs

Shreeram Garden & Banquets is one of the most renowned
wedding venues in Kolkata, located on main VIP road, near
Exide R&D / Teghoria crossing, making it most accessible and
convenient for all your guests.

It has multiple large air-conditioned banquet halls with
adjoining lush green lawns. The banquets, lawns and common
areas together add up to more than 50,000 sq ft and there are
multiple options for both large and small events. The interiors
of the banquets are lavish and include furniture - chairs, tables
and sofas. Our lawns are well cobbled and carpeted with
natural grass while the outdoor dining area is tiled with Kota
stone and has the option of artificial grass carpeting.
